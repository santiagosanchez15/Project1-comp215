HELLO, HOW THE TEAM SPOKE
A Novel in Conversations

============================================================

There is a particular kind of team that forms around a difficult problem. Not the kind assembled by org charts and job descriptions, but the kind that assembles itself, slowly, through shared frustration and mutual respect and the reliable ritual of asking each other how they are doing and meaning it.

This is the story of that kind of team.
